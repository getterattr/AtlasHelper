# Atlas Questline - PoE 3.28+

Facts about atlas voidstone progression and Maven mechanics in Path of Exile 3.28 (Mirage) and later.

## Voidstones

Four voidstones, one per atlas corner. Each is dropped by a distinct encounter, and each must be **socketed into its corner slot on the atlas** to take effect.

Reaching a corner slot to socket a voidstone requires unlocking a path of adjacent maps: an atlas node becomes reachable once one of its connected neighbours has been completed. On a fresh atlas, only T1 nodes are initially reachable; the completed-frontier expands outward from there as the player completes maps.

The four corners:

| Corner | Voidstone | Source encounter |
|---|---|---|
| Top-left | Originator | Incarnation of Dread (final boss of Eagon's memory questline) |
| Bottom-left | Eldritch | Searing Exarch and Eater of Worlds (both required) |
| Top-right | Decayed | The Shaper and The Elder (both required) |
| Bottom-right | Ceremonial | The Maven (via The Maven's Writ) |

## Originator - Eagon's memory questline

- **Eagon Caeserius** is first encountered on the first **T14+** map the player runs, starting the *Threads of the Originator* questline.
- The questline chains through three T16 memory guardian maps - **Courtyard of Wasting**, **Chambers of Impurity**, and **Theatre of Lies** - reached by talking to Eagon directly to progress each stage.
- Completing all three guardians opens the **Incarnation of Dread** fight.
- Defeating the Incarnation drops the Originator Voidstone.

## Eldritch - Searing Exarch and Eater of Worlds

Both pinnacles must be defeated for the Eldritch Voidstone. Each has an independent quest chain; the voidstone drops once both chains are complete.

Both chains begin the same way: encounter **The Envoy** in a yellow map (T6+), who introduces the pinnacle and hands over its unlock item.

### Searing Exarch chain

1. Envoy hands over the **Luminous Astrolabe**. Interacting with the Map Device consumes it and adds a Searing Exarch influence toggle.
2. Add Searing Exarch influence to a **T11+ map** and kill the boss. The boss drops the **Polaric Invitation**.
3. Use the Polaric Invitation in the Map Device to fight **The Black Star**.
4. After The Black Star is defeated, run Exarch-influenced maps at progressively higher tiers (T12, T13, T14, T15, T16), toggling influence via the Map Device each time.
5. The T16 Exarch-influenced boss drops the **Incandescent Invitation**.
6. Use the Incandescent Invitation to fight **The Searing Exarch**.

### Eater of Worlds chain

1. Envoy hands over the **Flesh Compass**. Interacting with the Map Device consumes it and adds an Eater of Worlds influence toggle.
2. Ramp Eater influence through T9+, T10+, T12+, and up to T16+ maps, running the required count at each stage.
3. The T16 Eater-influenced boss drops the **Screaming Invitation**.
4. Use the Screaming Invitation to fight **The Eater of Worlds**.

Eldritch altars spawn on monster corpses in influenced maps throughout both chains; each altar offers a paired upside/downside modifier choice. They are optional to the questline but affect map difficulty and rewards.

Once both chains are complete, the Eldritch Voidstone is granted.

## Decayed - The Shaper and The Elder

Both bosses must be defeated for the Decayed Voidstone. The base fights are sufficient in 3.28+.

- Collect fragments for both fights through Guardian farming: Shaper Guardians (Chimera, Hydra, Minotaur, Phoenix) drop Shaper fragments; Elder Guardians (Enslaver, Constrictor, Purifier, Eradicator) drop Elder fragments.
- Fight The Shaper. Fight The Elder.
- Voidstone drops.

## Maven's Beacon (prerequisite for all Maven invitations)

**Maven's Beacon** is a quest item awarded after meeting The Envoy and unlocking the first Watchstone altar. It adds a **toggle to the Map Device**: enabling it invites the Maven to witness the boss of that map. Killing the boss while she witnesses registers it toward invitation prerequisites.

Source:
- [The Maven's Beacon | PoE Wiki](https://pathofexile.fandom.com/wiki/The_Maven's_Beacon)

## Maven's Invitation: The Atlas

A five-stage quest chain. Each stage requires the player to have the Maven witness N different map bosses at the minimum tier, after which **Kirac opens the invitation** in his hideout to fight all N bosses together in the Maven's Crucible.

| Stage | Quest step | Witness | Min map tier | Invitation opened |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Collect more Map Bosses for the Maven | 3 different map bosses | T6+ | 3-way |
| 2 | Collect more Map Bosses for the Maven 2 | 4 different map bosses | T8+ | 4-way |
| 3 | Collect more Map Bosses for the Maven 3 | 5 different map bosses | T10+ | 5-way |
| 4 | Collect more Map Bosses for the Maven 4 | 6 different map bosses | T12+ | 6-way |
| 5 | Collect more Map Bosses for the Maven 5 | 10 different map bosses | T14+ | 10-way (repeatable) |

No 8-way exists. After stage 5 the questline is complete and the 10-way remains as the recurring splinter source.

Rewards per invitation:
- ~3-4 **Crescent Splinters**.
- First completion of each stage: 1 Atlas Passive Point + 2 refund points.

## Ceremonial - The Maven

1. Combine 10 Crescent Splinters into **The Maven's Writ**.
2. Use the Writ in the Map Device. Solo fight The Maven in *Absence of Mercy and Empathy*.
3. Ceremonial Voidstone drops.

Sources:
- [The Maven | PoE Wiki](https://www.poewiki.net/wiki/The_Maven)
- [The Maven's Writ | PoE Wiki](https://www.poewiki.net/wiki/The_Maven's_Writ)

## Themed Maven's Invitations

Additional Maven Crucible invitations opened by Kirac once the requisite bosses have been witnessed. All drop **Crescent Splinters** and feed the same Writ economy as the Atlas invitations. Ran before the Maven fight.

Delivery: enable **Maven's Beacon** in the Map Device, complete each required boss fight or Guardian map in that state. Once the invitation's full witness set is complete, Kirac makes the invitation available in his hideout.

| Invitation | Boss composition |
|---|---|
| The Formed | 4 Shaper Guardians: Chimera, Hydra, Minotaur, Phoenix |
| The Twisted | 4 Elder Guardians: Enslaver, Constrictor, Purifier, Eradicator |
| The Elderslayers | 4 Conquerors: Baran, Veritania, Al-Hezmin, Drox |
| The Forgotten | 4 Synthesis bosses including Rewritten Synthete; drops Cortex map |
| The Remembered | 3 memory-thread bosses: Courtyard of Wasting, Chambers of Impurity, Theatre of Lies |
| The Feared | 5 endgame bosses simultaneously: The Shaper, The Elder (Red), Cortex's Synthete Nightmare, Sirus (Eye of the Storm), and the Incarnation of Dread (Moment of Reverence). All phase transitions removed. |

## Bonus completion targets

- **Normal maps**: 100 bonus points. One per atlas-tree map boss killed at native tier.
- **Unique maps**: 10 bonus points, capped. **1 point per first-completion**, credited for the **first 10 unique maps** completed. There are ~20 unique maps in the game (distinctive orange icons on the atlas); completions beyond the tenth grant no additional bonus point. Any 10 uniques satisfy the cap - the player may skip the ones they dislike.

Source ([GAMES.GG Mirage Atlas Guide](https://games.gg/path-of-exile/guides/path-of-exile-mirage-league-atlas-guide/)) verbatim:

> You earn 10 points from Unique Maps, and there are around 20 Unique Maps available, with you only needing to complete 10, so you can skip the ones you dislike.
